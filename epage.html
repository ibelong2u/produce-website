The Artemis Story

Artemis Farms is a part of the growing Artemis family which provides berry-based ingredients to the functional food, beverage, and nutraceutical industries. Artemis Farms is the growing division of Artemis – working not only on our own berry growing operations but directly with other berry growers to provide support and resources as needed. Artemis Farms is committed to providing ingredients with US sourced berries both supporting our growers and assuring our customers of the source of our raw materials.
