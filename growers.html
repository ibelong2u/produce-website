Working with Artemis

Artemis is committed to working closely with growers in the US to bring aronia cultivation back to North America where it all began. Part of our vision is to collaborate with growers and have a forum to share knowledge about what we know about aronia. Artemis has been working with aronia since the mid 1990's and we have been referred to in the ingredient business as "the chokeberry people". We stay informed on the crops in Europe, what is being grown, growing conditions, harvest updates, the market in Europe and the US, where the science is and where it's heading, we work with researchers on aronia-based studies and with integrative health practitioners on where the science is and how aronia can help their patients.

Resources for Growers

We want to provide a forum for growers to not only learn from each other but to be able to ask questions of us as the need arises. Our "grower blog" will hopefully allow us enter into conversations about aronia, types of cultivars being grown, ages & yields of plants, if anyone is struggling with pests, fungus, any issues when growing organically, fertilizers recommended and those to stay away from, to irrigate or not to irrigate, which harvester is best and any challenges with using a harvester on immature plants, etc. We all want to see aronia become a successful crop in the US and we hope this blog is one way to see that happen.

Artemis will be sending out at first quarterly and then monthly newsletters to keep the growers updated on all things aronia - from cultivar research to recommendations for growing to what research we are involved in. Please join our mailing list to get the latest e-newsletters and if you have anything you would like to share in a newsletter please email your ideas or writings at blog@artemisfarms.com
